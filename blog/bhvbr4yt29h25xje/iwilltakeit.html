my friends


of course the way things are had to end eventually. the harsh reality is that i won't see a lot of you ever again. it's sad, but our lives our too short to be concerned with everyday tragedies.

of course, this means leaving my impression of you in an imperfect state. this is something i'm still coming to terms with. i'll get there eventually.

in the meantime, i want to form an agreement that you keep me in some corner of your mind. i have so many ideas, and very few escape and become fully formed. but if you keep me there, and wait patiently, i promise that someday i'll have something new to share with you all.

i honestly don't know if anyone is invested and capable enough to make it this far. if you are reading this, know that you are almost at the summit, but remember to enjoy the climb :)

be careful,
malachi
